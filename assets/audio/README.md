# Audio Folder

Place your Christmas music file here.

## Recommended:
- **Filename:** `music.mp3` (or update the path in `index.html`)
- **File size:** Under 5MB for reasonable loading time
- **Format:** MP3 (best browser support) or OGG
- **Duration:** 1-3 minutes is ideal

## Where to find free Christmas music:
- [Free Music Archive](https://freemusicarchive.org/search?quicksearch=christmas)
- [YouTube Audio Library](https://www.youtube.com/audiolibrary) (download audio tracks)
- [Incompetech](https://incompetech.com/music/royalty-free/music.html) (filter by Christmas)
- [Bensound](https://www.bensound.com/)

## Important:
- Ensure you have the right to use the music
- Check licensing requirements (attribution, commercial use, etc.)
- Most browsers require user interaction before autoplay works

## Tips:
- Choose instrumental music or appropriate lyrics
- Consider volume levels - not too loud!
- Test on different devices before sharing
